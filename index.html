About RAISE 2026

Vision & Goal

Hands‑on workshop merging Agnetic Robotics (LLM-driven) and VLA models for sustainable agriculture. The robot autonomously inspects greenhouses, generates field reports, and acts via natural language.

Dates & Venue

14–16 July 2026 · ISGIS, Tunisia.
Organized by RESDA (Renewable Energy & Sustainable Development Association).
